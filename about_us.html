vision:

A vision is a clear, comprehensive 'photograph' of an organization at some point in the future. It provides direction because it describes what the organization needs to be like, to be successful within the future. A vision describes the WHAT. i.e. what you are trying to achieve in the future.

mission:

A mission statement defines what an organization is, why it exists, its reason for being. At a minimum, your mission statement should define who your primary customers are, identify the products and services you produce, and describe the geographical location in which you operate.
